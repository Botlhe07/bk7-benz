    • 1886 - The First Gasoline Car

    Karl Benz creates the Benz Patent-Motorwagen, pioneering modern automobiles.

    1926 - Formation of Mercedes-Benz

    The merger of Benz & Cie and Daimler-Motoren-Gesellschaft officially forms their first branded car.

    1954 - The 300SL Gullwing

    The Mercedes-Benz 300SL is introduced, featuring iconic gullwing doors.

    1971 - Anti-lock Braking System (ABS)

    Mercedes-Benz debuts ABS technology, revolutionizing vehicle safety introducing it as a feature in their S-Class models in 1978.

    1998 - First Luxury SUV

    The Mercedes-Benz ML-Class becomes the brand’s first SUV model.

    2019 - Entering the EV Market

    The Mercedes-Benz EQC launches, marking their commitment to electric vehicles.

    2021 - The EQS Flagship Sedan

    The Mercedes-Benz EQS debuts as a fully electric luxury sedan.
